Opening Sun Homes BBQ with many attractive incentives

Posted on 06/02/2018 by anhnguyen

On April 26, the king of Korean barbecue - King BBQ will continue his "travel" to 109 Dao Tan. Celebrating this special event, King BBQ Dao Tan offers customers 5,000 table booking codes with a 20% discount.

Located in the middle of a street known as "Japanese street", the king of Korean barbecue - King BBQ Dao Tan is the first King BBQ restaurant to be franchised in Hanoi - to bring a new breeze of cuisine here to the diners. visitors come by.

Still maintaining the outstanding features that make up its brand, coming to King BBQ Dao Tan, customers can not only enjoy dishes with rich flavors and culinary style of Kim Chi but also feel the space. Korean culture in the service style and restaurant space decoration style.

The difference in King BBQ Dao Tan's menu is that the restaurant will serve both buffet and a la carte options. You can choose to eat all you want with buffet levels such as 199,000 VND, 279,000 VND, 329,000 VND and 349,000 VND or dine a la carte. With a menu of nearly 200 premium dishes that diners cannot miss such as: King BBQ Beef Belly, Samyeopsal Pork Belly, Guang Yang Beef Sauce,... or typical Korean dishes such as Bibimbab Mixed Rice, Beef Rib Soup , Kimchi, Bulgogi are also always ready to serve you.

Welcome brother King BBQ Dao Tan to join the Korean BBQ King family, with a series of extremely hot promotions that you cannot miss if you are a fan of Kim Chi cuisine.

  • Get 5,000 20% discount codes immediately when customers book a table via Landingpage or hotline: 04.6688 1828, especially applicable for holidays.
  • For groups of 3 or more, receive a voucher of 100 King VND when booking in advance via Hotline: 04.6688 1828
