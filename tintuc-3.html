Sun Homes BBQ jubilantly opened to celebrate the new year 2018

Posted on 06/02/2018 by anhnguyen

Red Sun International Trade Investment Joint Stock Company (REDSUN ITI) has just officially opened King BBQ Buffet restaurant - King of Korean BBQ at 310 Tan Son Nhi, Tan Son Nhi ward, Tan Phu district, City. HCM with the desire to continue serving diners who love unique Korean cuisine.

Along with many other systems nationwide, King BBQ Buffet is an à la carte buffet model, giving diners the experience of authentic Korean grilled cuisine. With quality food, attentive service and reasonable prices, King BBQ Buffer quickly won the hearts of many diners. Besides premium imported American beef and fresh seafood, marinated according to secret recipes and using a modern smokeless grill system right at the table, diners can freely explore countless dishes. Love the great flavors on King BBQ's buffet counter. The menu is rich and diverse, prepared by skillful and meticulous hands, combined with special seasoning techniques of chef Park Sung Min with over 40 years of experience, and a luxurious space. Modern, King BBQ Buffet is an ideal meeting place for those who are passionate about rich, flavorful Korean barbecue.

During the opening month, King BBQ Buffet Tan Son Nhi will offer diners many attractive incentives: 01 free buffet at the same price for a group of 04 guests (charged for 03 meals). For every bill of 500,000 VND or more and using Strongbow products, customers will receive a unique water bottle. Free fresh soft drinks for customers checking in to the restaurant location on King BBQ fanpage.

Food court at King BBQ Buffet

It is known that Red Sun International Trade Investment Joint Stock Company (REDSUN ITI) is currently one of the leading culinary companies in Vietnam, with more than 12 middle and high-end culinary brands: King BBQ, Hotpot Story, Sushi Kei, Seoul Garden, Tasaki BBQ, ThaiExpress, Khao Lao, Capricciosa... With the goal of becoming a pioneer in the field of restaurant chain business, REDSUN ITI currently has more than 160 restaurants spanning from North to South, always upholding and committing to the mission of bringing customers high-end culinary brands, luxury restaurant chains, excellent dishes and classy services.
